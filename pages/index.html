    About
    One day I accidentally discovered Flaming Skate Arena. Done modest few kilometers there and realised that some Fernradwege can be done with rollerskates, too. And I don't even mean 100mm pro wheels. Soon after Google Maps added "bicycle paths" layer which made trip-planning easier, in combo with occasional street-view. This helps to assess missing bikepath segments. So the long-distance rollerskating started, spiralling outwards from Berlin, region after region.
